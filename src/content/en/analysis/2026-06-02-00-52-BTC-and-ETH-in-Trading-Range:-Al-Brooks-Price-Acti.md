## Risk Considerations

1. **False Breakouts**: In a trading range, breakout signals can be traps. Wait for a second confirmation (e.g., a retest of support/resistance) before entering.
2. **Macro Factors**: Monitor global economic events (e.g., interest rate decisions, regulatory news) that could trigger sudden volatility and break the range.
3. **Position Sizing**: Use lighter positions in range-bound markets. Place stops based on price action levels, not fixed pips.
4. **Time of Day**: Low liquidity during weekends or holidays can reduce signal reliability. Prioritize trading during Asian or European/American sessions.
